Word Embedding & Text Classification

Learn the basics of how word embedding and text classification work in natural language processing (NLP).

What is Word Embedding?

Word embedding is a technique where words or phrases are mapped to vectors of real numbers. This allows words with similar meanings to have similar representations. Popular methods include Word2Vec and GloVe.

Example of Word Embedding

In Word2Vec, words like 'king' and 'queen' are similar because their vector representations are close in the embedding space.

What is Text Classification?

Text classification is a process where text is categorized into predefined categories. It is widely used in spam detection, sentiment analysis, and topic classification.
